Paid Family and Medical Leave (PFML)

Client: State paid leave authority

Role: Led research and design efforts.

Goal

Launch a user-friendly digital experience to educate and enroll employers and employees in a new benefits program.

Approach

  • Conducted 20+ interviews and focus groups
  • Developed personas and journey maps
  • Designed guided user journeys tailored to each group
  • Validated through usability testing before development

Impact

139,000+ businesses registered and significant fund balance achieved.
